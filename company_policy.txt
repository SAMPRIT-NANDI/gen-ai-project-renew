COMPANY POLICY DOCUMENT

1. WORK HOURS
- Standard work hours are 9 AM to 5 PM, Monday through Friday.
- Employees are expected to be at their desks and ready to work by 9 AM.
- Flexible hours are available upon manager approval.

2. LEAVE POLICY
- Employees are entitled to 20 days of paid leave per year.
- Sick leave requests must be submitted within 24 hours when possible.
- Maternity leave is 6 months with full pay for the first 3 months.
- Paternity leave is 2 weeks with full pay.

3. REMOTE WORK
- Remote work is allowed up to 3 days per week with manager approval.
- Employees must maintain regular communication with their team.
- Remote work is not permitted on crucial meeting days without prior permission.

4. CODE OF CONDUCT
- All employees must treat colleagues with respect and professionalism.
- Discrimination and harassment of any kind are strictly prohibited.
- Confidential company information must never be shared without authorization.
- Violations of this policy may result in termination.

5. TRAINING AND DEVELOPMENT
- The company provides annual training budgets of up to $2,000 per employee.
- Employees are encouraged to attend relevant workshops and conferences.
- Professional certifications are supported as part of career development.

6. PERFORMANCE REVIEWS
- Annual performance reviews are conducted in December.
- Employees receive feedback on their performance and goals for the next year.
- Salary adjustments are discussed during performance reviews.

7. HEALTH AND SAFETY
- The company maintains a safe working environment.
- All employees must follow health and safety guidelines.
- Workplace injuries must be reported immediately to HR.

8. DRESS CODE
- Business casual is the standard dress code for office days.
- Formal business attire is required for client meetings and presentations.
- Remote work days have a casual dress code policy.
